What is Sparsh?
Sparsh is not just a VR glove - it’s a whole new way to feel the digital world. Designed for next-gen virtual interaction, Sparsh lets your hands do more than just wave or point. Now, you can reach out, grab, press, and actually feel virtual objects as if they were real. Sparsh understands the natural movement of your hand - from every finger bend to subtle gestures - and brings that precision into VR. Your virtual hand mirrors your real one in real time.

Features

Real-Time Finger Movement Detection
Each glove is embedded with precision potentiometers that monitor the flexion of the PIP, DIP, and MCP joints in real-time. This allows the system to accurately mirror complex hand gestures and natural finger articulation, creating a responsive and intuitive interaction model in virtual space.
Precision Force-Feedback Technology
Sparsh delivers finely-tuned resistance to simulate the weight, texture, and solidity of virtual objects. Whether you're holding a tool, pressing a button, or feeling the tension of a rope, each interaction is rendered with lifelike accuracy, enabling intuitive and immersive experiences.
Full-Body Motion Tracking with Jelly Trackers
Complementing the glove, the Jelly Tracker system uses a network of five lightweight IMU-based sensors (two on the arms, two on the legs, and one on the chest) to track full-body movement. This allows users to move naturally within virtual environments, with their physical motion reflected accurately and seamlessly in VR.
Synchronized Glove + Body Integration
The integration of Sparsh with the Jelly Tracker system provides holistic, full-body immersion. Hand gestures, body posture, and limb movement are captured and translated simultaneously, allowing for advanced use cases in training, simulation, design, and interactive storytelling.

Your questions answered

What is Sparsh?
Sparsh is a force-feedback VR glove that lets you feel virtual objects with realistic resistance.
Do I need anything else to use the Jelly Trackers?
Just a PC and VR headset. No base stations or cameras - the trackers are fully self-contained.
Can I use this with games on Steam?
Yes! Once set up, you can use Sparsh and the trackers in most SteamVR games - especially those with hand or body interactions.
What is the setup process like?
Plug in via USB or pair with Bluetooth, run the OpenGloves driver, and you’re good to go.
How many trackers do I need for full-body movement?
The more the merrier! But we recommend using 5 - two for arms, two for legs, and one on the chest.
Can I use only the glove or only the trackers?
Yes. They're fully modular - you can mix and match based on your needs.
Is the software open-source?
Yes! Firmware, drivers, and SDKs are fully open-source and customizable.
What if I want to tweak or build my own version?
Go for it. We built this for hackers, devs, and creators - everything’s yours to modify.
